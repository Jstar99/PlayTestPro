Welcome to PlayTestPro!

Building better software together! join us as we simplify the development process by taking the guess work out of building products.

Who we are

We are a company of developers who have seen and experienced first-hand the struggles of finding a significant number of willing participants to provide feedback on our projects. We found that current solutions were just not accommodating to early-stage developers/products, leading us to the conclusion that there must be a better way.

What we do

Our goal and mission are to arm developers at any stage of their software cycle with the tools and information necessary to build the best version of their product.

How it works

Step 1: Sign Up

Create an account on our site by filling out a simple form. We'll ask for some basic information to get you started whether you are a developer or tester.

Step 2: Pick an App

Browse our list of available games and apps that are available for feedback. We'll provide you with instructions on how to download and install the app.

Step 3: Give Feedback

Use the selected app for a few minutes and provide us with your honest feedback. We'll ask you some questions about your experience and provide you with a rating system to help us evaluate the app after which you will be rewarded with an incentive.
